--- 4. Ambiguous city ---
User: I want to plan an event in Springfield for 20 people with a budget of $30 each.
Tool Calls: [
  {
    "name": "get_weather",
    "args": "{\"city\":\"Springfield\"}",
    "result": {
      "city": "Springfield",
      "country": "United States",
      "precipitation_chance": 51
    }
  },
  {
    "name": "calculate_event_cost",
    "args": "{\"headcount\":20,\"base_budget\":30,\"weather_risk\":true}",
    "result": {
      "headcount": 20,
      "base_budget": 30,
      "backup_cost_delta": 9.0,
      "weather_risk": true,
      "final_per_person_cost": 39.0,
      "total_cost": 780.0
    }
  }
]
Plan Summary Rendered: {
  "city": "Springfield",
  "headcount": 20,
  "base_budget": 30,
  "backup_cost_delta": 9,
  "weather_risk": true,
  "final_per_person_cost": 39,
  "recommendation": "Proceed with planning, considering the weather risk."
}
Assistant: The weather in Springfield has a 51% chance of precipitation, indicating a weather risk for your event. Given your budget of $30 per person for 20 people, the total cost will be $780. This includes an additional contingency of $9 per person due to the weather risk, raising the final per-person cost to $39.

Would you like to proceed with planning, or do you have any questions?
